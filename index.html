Overview

My project will be a responsive learning English website with free real IELTS exams, IELTS tips and numerous innovative features to make IELTS test easier, helping everybody improve their IELTS scores. This web would be a good preparation for them to practice online test like real tests. People will find the tips that show them some ways to get high score and how to make an IELT test. Furthermore, website have a variety of games for students not only to entertain but also to widen their vocabulary reservoir. There are several online courses and live session for advice from the experts.

Motivation

IELTS is one of the world’s most popular high-stakes English language tests, with over three million tests taken in 2018, and is recognized by more than 10,000 universities, schools, employers and immigration bodies, including all universities in Australia and the UK and many of the leading institutions in the USA. RMIT University also requires that students who want to study in there must have IELTS score. Therefore, grade 12 students usually prepare for this exam and face many difficulties in learning IELTS, because they spend more 8 hours per day at school and they do not have many times left if they go to English center. My website is created to solve this problem since students could use it to practice 4 skills of IELTS whenever and wherever they want.

Description

This web contains many pages:

Index page: Users have to sign up and sign in to use this web. It is free for everybody to create an account or they could use Google account or Facebook instead. This account will help IELTS test takers to track their IELTS learning and set their goals. They could view all data like average IELTS band scores and time spent. It even shows what type of questions learners need to improve on and for which elements they need to study more.

Reading skills page: This page has hundreds of IELTS reading test from elementary to advance. The IELTS reading passage and question paper are organized side-by-side to provide more convenience and a better testing experience. It's like having the two papers on your desk. Learners could take note or highlight within reading passages, which is easier for them to do the test. There is a timer clock on the top of the page for them to make sure them submit test on time. When they run out of time, the web automatically saves and submit it to system. Once their test is submitted, their band score will be calculated corresponding with the number of correct answered questions. This web also provides explanations for their incorrect answers.

Listening skills page: This page has the same feature with Reading skills page. A small difference is that there would be a audio media players to play listening tests.

Writing skills page: Writing test library has hundreds of various topics ranging from discussions to agree/disagree with someone’s view. All of which are real tests collected by experts and students who have taken the test. After users finish their essay and submit it, the test will be marked by well-qualified staff. In addition, learners could view the essay of other student having high score, which could make quickly improve their writing skill.

Speaking skills page: Learns who want to practice speaking could make an appointment with staff. Then, they could do sample tests with experts through Skype.

Discussion forum page: In this forum, every user could post their questions and the answer would be contributed by fellow students and teachers.

Game page: there are many minigames for students to entertain and study. For example, Flashcards games contain some new words with clear definition and picture, which may help players efficiently cultivate knowledge.

Skills Required:

The most important skill for developing a website is the basics of coding and markup language such as HTML/CSS. For the backend development, I need to understand PHP, which could make my website dynamic. To manage database, I should know about SQL. In the Internet age, it would be very easy for me to gain these skills through online courses, videos, documents.

Outcome:

I will try my best to make this website. If this project is successful, everybody can practice a large number of IELTS test anytime and anywhere. This means that students do not need to travel to English center and make appointment for learning. Moreover, people could know about their learning process through analysis system of web which evaluates their reading, writing, speaking, and listening skills. I hope that my website could help everyone for their own practice and have a good IELTS score.
